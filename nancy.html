SDE at Amazon India

As soon as I hear the word "IIT Ropar", thousands of memories come across my mind and take me to the place where I spent the best time of my life.

Hi all, I am Nancy Balhara, an Electrical Engineering B. Tech. graduate of 2017 Batch. I am currently an SDE in Amazon.

IIT Ropar has been a home to me with a family of more than 600 people since the last four years. I have found some very nice friends here and I will cherish these friendships forever. Three words to describe this place - Opportunities, Opportunities, and Opportunities.

When I joined IIT Ropar, I was a completely different person - I was shy, introvert and completely clueless about pretty much everything..... This place transformed me into a confident, strong and better person. Be it Academics, sports, music or any other extracurricular activity the facilities provided by the institution are fantabulous. The faculty is great with full of enthusiasm, the labs are well equipped, there are talented coaches for sports and the best part is there are a lot of clubs and societies like astronomy club, Robotics, Dance Club, Music Club, Enactus and many more where one can grow and learn. There are thousands of opportunities!! One can always pursue his/her dreams here. The seniors, alumni, faculty all are very supportive and always there to help. The juniors are very friendly and the batch mates are always with you.

Advice for the juniors... Enjoy every moment, don't overburden yourselves during exam only.. Study continuously.. Atleast attend the classes.. Grab every opportunity you get... Join sports... Do internships.. It provides you exposure. Do projects with willpower and try to learn different things instead of cramming . Set a goal for yourself.. And work towards it along with your regular curriculum.

Ps. Don't waste your time watching TV series and playing video games.. You can do that anytime... Be with your friends.. Try to learn from each other... Everyone of you is special

12 Jan 2018
